Our story

Stillness, held in wood

VANYRA is not about volume. It is about presence — the way a bowl rests on a table, the way bamboo hums under the hand.

We are a small studio in the Dang region, where forest and craft meet. Wood and bamboo arrive with their own character; we listen more than we impose.

Philosophy: fewer objects, longer relationships. If something leaves our bench, it should earn its place in your home.
